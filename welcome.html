👋 Welcome to Automation Pro Workflow

Automation Pro is your personal AI video assistant — a plug-and-play automation system that clones TikTok videos from your Google Drive and posts them for you, completely hands-free.

⚙️ What It Does

  • 🎬 Downloads videos from your Google Drive folder
  • 🚀 Uploads them to TikTok automatically
  • 📅 Posts once per day (or on demand)
  • 🧠 Runs on your own computer — no coding needed

💡 Why It’s Powerful

  • 🔒 100% Local – Runs from your device. No 3rd parties.
  • 💸 Free Forever – No monthly fees. No post limits.
  • 🔁 Clone-Ready – Repost trending videos and grow on autopilot.
  • 🧱 Fully Customizable – Change captions, timing, sources, and more.

🚀 Installation Steps

  1. Download Docker Desktop: https://www.docker.com/products/docker-desktop/
  2. Open your Terminal or Command Prompt
  3. Paste and run:
    docker run -it --rm -p 5678:5678 -v ~/.n8n:/home/node/.n8n n8nio/n8n
  4. Go to http://localhost:5678
  5. Import the included Automation Pro Workflow.json
  6. Connect your Google Drive + TikTok (API key or sid_tt cookie)
  7. Click “Execute Workflow” to test
  8. Use a Cron node to automate daily posting
